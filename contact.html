Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know.

Do I need any prior trading experience to join?
Absolutely not. The Foundation program is designed specifically for complete beginners — we start from scratch. If you know nothing about forex, candlestick charts, or financial markets, that's exactly who BiddyFX Foundation is built for. We take you from zero to confident, step by step.
How quickly can I get access after signing up?
Instant. Create a free account on our student portal and you're in immediately. The Foundation course and all student tools — including the position calculator, session clock, and community access — are available the moment your account is created. No waiting, no approval process.
What equipment do I need to start?
Just a smartphone or laptop and an internet connection. We recommend completing the course on a desktop or laptop for the best experience. For live trading practice, we recommend MetaTrader 4 (MT4) — it's free to download and works on both desktop and mobile. Your broker will provide you with a demo account.
Is there a mobile app for the student dashboard?
Our student portal is fully mobile-responsive and works beautifully on any device through your mobile browser. Simply visit biddyfx.com/login on your phone's browser, save it to your home screen, and it functions just like an app. Native iOS/Android apps are on our roadmap.
What's included in the free Foundation program?
The Foundation includes 18 full lessons across 4 modules: Forex Fundamentals (how the market works, currency pairs, candlestick charts, lots and leverage), Support & Resistance basics, an introduction to Breakout Trading, and Trendline fundamentals. You also get access to the student dashboard, position size calculator, trading session clock, and our private community Discord.
What does Pro Trader unlock?
Pro Trader is the full BiddyFX system. It includes everything in Foundation plus: advanced breakout trading with fakeout filters, complete trendline mastery, weekly live trading sessions where you watch and trade alongside our analyst in real time, London and NY signal alerts, a 1-on-1 strategy call, and access to our private Pro mentorship group on WhatsApp/Telegram.
What is the Apex / Funded Trader program?
Apex is for traders who want to pass prop firm challenges and trade with funded accounts (FTMO, MyForexFunds, The Funded Trader, etc.). You get everything in Pro Trader plus: a personalized trading plan built around your specific prop firm's rules, monthly 1-on-1 coaching sessions, and access to our exclusive Apex mastermind group. Contact us to apply — spots are limited.
Can I upgrade from Foundation to Pro later?
Yes, absolutely. Start free with Foundation — there's no time limit. When you're ready to level up (or when your demo account results prove you're ready for the full system), reach out to us via WhatsApp or the contact form below and we'll get you set up within 24 hours. Your existing progress is saved and carries over.
How much money do I need to start live trading?
We strongly recommend starting on a demo account and spending at least 30–60 days there before touching real money. When you do go live, you can start with as little as $100–$500 using micro lots. However, to trade meaningfully with proper risk management (1% risk per trade), we recommend a minimum of $500–$1,000. Never trade money you cannot afford to lose.
Which broker do you recommend?
We don't officially endorse any specific broker, but we advise choosing one that is: regulated by a reputable authority (FCA, ASIC, CySEC), offers MT4/MT5, has tight spreads on the pairs you want to trade, and has solid customer support. For Nigerian traders, popular choices include Exness, HFM, and IC Markets. Always verify regulation before depositing funds.
Do you provide trading signals?
Pro Trader and Apex members receive trade setup alerts during the London and New York sessions. However, our primary focus is teaching you to find your own setups — signals alone don't make consistent traders; understanding makes consistent traders. We provide signals as a learning supplement, not a substitute for developing your own skills.
How do I pay for Pro or Apex programs?
After you apply or express interest through the contact form or WhatsApp, we'll send you payment details. We accept bank transfer (Nigerian banks), USDT/crypto, and PayPal where available. Once payment is confirmed, your access is upgraded within 24 hours. Contact us via WhatsApp for the fastest response.
Is there a refund policy?
We offer a 7-day satisfaction guarantee for Pro Trader. If you've completed less than 30% of the Pro content and are genuinely unsatisfied within 7 days of purchase, contact us and we'll process your refund. Apex program refunds are handled case-by-case given the personalized nature of the program. Foundation is completely free, so no refund applies.
Are there payment plans available?
Yes. We want access to be achievable for serious students regardless of financial situation. Flexible installment plans are available for both Pro Trader and Apex — typically 2–3 monthly payments. Contact us via WhatsApp to discuss options. We assess each case individually and find something that works.
Still have questions?

Our team typically responds within 2 hours during business hours. Reach out on WhatsApp for the fastest response.
